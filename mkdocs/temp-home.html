Introducing Visivo
Version controlled, testable and modular data visualization.

Unlock the latent potential for your engineering and analytics teams to deliver high quality, maintainable, dashboards that power data driven deicions and enable customer facing in-app data visualization.

Powerful CLI

Simple and easy to use CLI enables fast local development, testability and quick deployments to remote enviornments. Drives integration with your existing CI process.

Customizable

Utilize 15+ different types of charts & interactive tables ensuring that you can choose the best visual represenation for you data possible. Customize everyting from front to color and much more.

Code Based

Write your visualizations in easy to maintain and modular yaml configurations that sit within your existing analytics transformations or application repository.

Built In Testing

Enable your team to move quickly and not break things with built in support for testing your data visualizations enabling faster iteration of downstream data without the fear of silently breaking internal dashboards and external application charts.

Integrates with your Existing Architecture

Setting up a CI, development and production process that mirrors your dbt core configuration is easy with the Visivo CLI and cloud deployemnts.
